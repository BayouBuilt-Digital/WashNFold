About the Program

More than laundry.

The Fresh Start Program is our way of giving back to the people who make this community what it is. Every month, Maurice Wash Co. awards two deserving community members with free laundry services — no strings attached.


Winners receive either three free self-service washes or two free Wash & Fold services. Because everyone deserves clean clothes, and no one in our community should have to go without.

Contest Rules

Official Rules.

01

Eligibility

The Fresh Start Program is open to residents of Maurice, Louisiana and the surrounding Vermilion Parish communities. Participants must be 18 years of age or older to submit a nomination.

02

How to Enter

Download and complete the official nomination form. Nominations may be submitted on behalf of yourself or another qualifying community member. Only one nomination per person per month will be accepted.

03

Winner Selection

The owners of Maurice Wash Co. will choose two lucky winners each month. Selections are made at the sole discretion of ownership and are based on demonstrated need and community spirit as described in the nomination. All decisions are final. The same individual may not be chosen as a winner more than once every year.

04

Prize

Each winner will receive their choice of either three (3) free self-service washes or two (2) free Wash & Fold services. Prizes have no cash value and are non-transferable. Services must be redeemed within 60 days of notification.

05

Notification

Winners will be contacted using the phone number or email address provided on their nomination form. If a winner cannot be reached within 7 days of selection, an alternate winner may be chosen.

06

Publicity & Consent

By accepting the prize, contest winners agree to have their name, photo, and story published on Maurice Wash Co.'s social media channels and website. This helps us celebrate our community and inspire others. Participation in publicity is a condition of accepting the prize.

07

Privacy

All nomination information is kept strictly confidential and will only be used for the purpose of the Fresh Start Program. Maurice Wash Co. will not share personal information with third parties.

08

General

Maurice Wash Co. reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue the Fresh Start Program at any time. Employees and immediate family members of Maurice Wash Co. are not eligible to participate.
