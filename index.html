About Us

Built for Maurice.
Built for you.

Maurice Wash Co. began the way most meaningful things do, with one woman who saw a need and decided to do something about it. We are currently in our pre-launch phase, working diligently to bring a much needed laundry service to Vermilion Parish.


Families in Maurice and the surrounding community of Milton are leaving their community just to find a washateria. That's not a minor inconvenience for working families, that's lost time and money they can't afford. Maurice Wash Co. is here to change that.

"For me, community means showing up, filling gaps, and making sure people don't fall through the cracks."

— Founder, Maurice Wash Co.

Our Story

Rooted in community.

Maurice Wash Co. was founded by a woman deeply rooted in community service. As a CASA volunteer — a Court Appointed Special Advocate for children in the welfare system — and someone who has spent years as an autism advocate, this business is a natural extension of that commitment.


For me, community means showing up, filling gaps, and making sure people don't fall through the cracks. Maurice Wash Co. is built on that same belief.
