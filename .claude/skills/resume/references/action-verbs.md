# Action verb bank

Use past tense for prior roles, present tense for current role only. Do not repeat the same verb across consecutive bullets — vary aggressively.

## Leadership and ownership
Led, Directed, Spearheaded, Championed, Owned, Drove, Orchestrated, Mobilized, Headed, Chaired, Founded, Established, Pioneered, Initiated, Mentored, Coached, Supervised, Managed, Oversaw, Delegated.

## Built / created
Built, Designed, Developed, Engineered, Architected, Created, Launched, Shipped, Released, Deployed, Implemented, Produced, Authored, Drafted, Prototyped, Constructed, Assembled, Configured.

## Improved / optimized
Improved, Optimized, Streamlined, Accelerated, Enhanced, Refined, Modernized, Upgraded, Reduced, Eliminated, Consolidated, Simplified, Automated, Refactored, Restructured, Standardized, Scaled, Decreased, Increased, Boosted, Doubled, Tripled.

## Analyzed / measured
Analyzed, Evaluated, Assessed, Audited, Diagnosed, Forecasted, Modeled, Quantified, Measured, Benchmarked, Investigated, Researched, Validated, Tested, Identified, Surfaced, Mapped, Tracked, Monitored, Reported.

## Sold / negotiated / persuaded
Sold, Closed, Negotiated, Pitched, Persuaded, Influenced, Secured, Won, Earned, Acquired, Converted, Onboarded, Retained, Upsold, Cross-sold, Renewed, Expanded.

## Delivered / shipped
Delivered, Executed, Completed, Finalized, Achieved, Surpassed, Exceeded, Met, Hit, Fulfilled, Resolved, Closed-out.

## Collaborated / coordinated
Collaborated, Partnered, Coordinated, Aligned, Liaised, Facilitated, Brokered, Negotiated, Unified, Consolidated, Bridged.

## Taught / communicated
Trained, Taught, Educated, Onboarded, Presented, Briefed, Communicated, Translated, Documented, Articulated, Demonstrated, Conveyed.

## Saved / cut costs
Saved, Cut, Reduced, Slashed, Eliminated, Decreased, Avoided, Recovered, Reclaimed, Negotiated-down.

## Verbs to avoid (weak / passive)
Helped, Assisted, Worked on, Was responsible for, Participated in, Involved in, Contributed to, Supported, Handled, Performed, Did, Made, Got, Used, Tried.

If a bullet starts with a "Verbs to avoid" item, rewrite it. "Helped reduce costs" → "Reduced costs by $X" or "Drove $X cost reduction across Y team."
