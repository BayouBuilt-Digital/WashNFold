# Bullet examples — before / after

The pattern: **Action verb + Task + Quantified result.** If you can add scope (team size, budget, user count) or timeframe, do it.

## Operations

**Before:** Responsible for managing the daily schedule of cleaners.
**After:** Built a routing model that re-sequenced 18 cleaners across 240 daily stops, cutting drive time 22% and saving $46K/yr in labor.

**Before:** Helped reduce errors in the order intake process.
**After:** Standardized order-intake SOP and rolled out training to 12 CSRs, cutting input errors 64% (from 38 to 14 per week) within 90 days.

## Customer experience

**Before:** Worked with customers to resolve complaints.
**After:** Resolved 95% of escalated complaints within one business day, lifting CSAT from 3.6 to 4.7/5 over two quarters.

**Before:** Improved customer onboarding.
**After:** Redesigned onboarding flow for 4,200+ new customers/yr, reducing first-week churn from 17% to 6% and adding ~$280K ARR.

## Engineering / technical

**Before:** Worked on the billing system.
**After:** Refactored billing service to event-driven architecture, eliminating nightly batch failures and cutting invoice latency from 14h to <30s.

**Before:** Helped migrate the database.
**After:** Led Postgres-to-Aurora migration for a 1.2 TB production database with zero downtime, reducing query p95 from 480ms to 110ms.

## Sales

**Before:** Sold subscriptions to customers.
**After:** Closed $1.4M in new ARR (118% of quota) by building outbound sequence into 80 mid-market accounts; ranked #2 of 14 reps.

## Data / analytics

**Before:** Built dashboards for the team.
**After:** Built executive Tableau dashboard surfacing 7 P&L drivers across 12 markets, replacing 4 manual reports and saving Finance 20+ hrs/wk.

## Project management

**Before:** Managed projects with multiple stakeholders.
**After:** Delivered 14 cross-functional projects ($3.2M total budget) on time and 8% under budget, coordinating 6 vendors and 4 internal teams.

## Process improvement

**Before:** Improved internal processes.
**After:** Mapped and re-engineered AP close process, cutting cycle from 9 days to 3 and freeing 0.5 FTE of analyst capacity.

## When you genuinely have no number

Lead with the strongest qualitative outcome.

**Weak:** Created a new training program for the team.
**Better:** Authored onboarding curriculum that became the template adopted org-wide across 4 regional offices.

**Weak:** Led the team through a difficult transition.
**Better:** Led 11-person team through ERP cutover with zero missed customer SLAs during the 6-week transition window.
