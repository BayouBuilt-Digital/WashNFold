Official Rules

Contest Rules.

01

Eligibility

The WashNFold Monthly Laundry Giveaway is open to all residents within the WashNFold service area (Maurice, Louisiana and surrounding Vermilion Parish communities). Participants must be 18 years of age or older to submit an entry. Employees and immediate family members of WashNFold and D & G Fuzion LLC are not eligible to enter or win.

02

How to Enter

Complete the official entry form on this page. You may nominate yourself or another person. Each entry must include the nominator's name, phone number, email, and address, plus a short essay explaining why the nominee deserves to win. Only one entry per person per month will be accepted. Duplicate entries will be disqualified.

03

Entry Deadline

All entries must be submitted by 11:59 PM CST on the 9th of each month. Late entries will be held for the following month's contest. Entries submitted after the deadline will not be considered for the current month.

04

Winner Selection

Entries will be posted for community voting in the WashNFold app. The entry with the most community votes will be declared the winner. In the event that no votes are cast during the voting period, the owners of WashNFold will select the winner at their sole discretion based on the strength of the nomination essay and demonstrated need. All decisions are final.

05

Winner Announcement

The winner will be announced by the 16th of each month via the WashNFold app. A personal message will also be sent directly to the winner using the contact information provided in their entry. If the winner cannot be reached within 7 days of announcement, an alternate winner may be chosen.

06

Prize

One (1) winner each month will receive one (1) free pickup and delivery laundry service for up to approximately 30 lbs of laundry. The prize has no cash value and is non-transferable. The service must be redeemed within 60 days of winner notification. The prize cannot be combined with other offers or promotions.

07

Publicity & Image Release

By accepting the prize, the winner grants WashNFold and D & G Fuzion LLC permission to use their name, photo, likeness, and story on the WashNFold website, social media channels (Facebook, Instagram, etc.), promotional materials, and the WashNFold app. This image and publicity release is a condition of accepting the prize. The winner's photo and story may be featured in the "Winner Spotlight" section of the contest page and the WashNFold homepage.

08

Privacy

All entry information is kept confidential and will only be used for the purposes of this contest and contacting the winner. WashNFold will not sell or share personal information with third parties. Nominee information will only be shared publicly if the nominee wins and accepts the prize.

09

Frequency & Repeat Winners

The contest runs monthly with no end date. The same individual may not win more than once within a 12-month period. Previous winners are welcome to nominate others. There is no limit to how many times a person may be nominated by different nominators.

10

General

WashNFold and D & G Fuzion LLC reserve the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue the Monthly Laundry Giveaway at any time without prior notice. By entering, all participants agree to be bound by these official rules. Any attempt to tamper with the entry process or voting will result in disqualification.
